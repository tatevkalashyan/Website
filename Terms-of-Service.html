The Registration Process
A Job Seeker is an individual looking for a shift or position with a Job Poster. A Job Seeker will create a profile and sign in through the Website or App. The profile will include certain information relevant to a potential employer such as name, address, professional license number, etc. ("Personal Information"). The Job Seeker can post “Contact Cards” so that a Job Poster may reach out about a potential position or shift.
A Job Poster is a potential employer that is looking to fill a temporary position or a shift at a medical office. The Job Poster will create an account in order to post “Shift Cards” which are advertising for open positions or shifts.
