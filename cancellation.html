If a Job Seeker is cancelling the shift, shift will automatically be reposted in the marketplace. If another match is successfully made, there will be no additional charge. In case no match is found, there will be full refund of the service fee.
To initiate refund process, Job Posters can submit a request through Help and Support page by selecting “Refund Request” from the dropdown. Please provide all necessary details in the text box.
