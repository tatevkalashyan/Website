Pricing

Temporary Placement Services:

A flat service fee is charged per shift. Job Seekers will be paid by Job Posters at an hourly rate agreed with Job Seeker on kolligate. The Job Poster is responsible for paying any applicable taxes, State, Federal, and Local, for the Job Seeker. The sole and only responsibility of Kolligate is to make the connection between the Job Seeker and Job Poster.

Assistants, Business Staff, Hygienists: $75
Dentists: $75
Permanent Placement Services
Permanent Placement Services

kolligate provides Job Posters the option to permanently hire Job Seekers who have worked with them for temp shifts in the past 12 months through the platform. Part time is 1-2 days/wk. Full-time is 3-5 days/wk. If a part time hire transitions to full time within 90 days, then the full-time fee is applicable. Fees listed apply to permanent employment only.

Office will get 10% discount when they reach out to Kolligate and inform about a permanent hire. If kolligate finds out and reaches out to the office then office will have to pay full fee.
