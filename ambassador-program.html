Kolligate Ignite Ambassadors
At kolligate , our goal is to build a marketplace for Dental professionals which is convenient to use and is transparent. We aim to enhance the experience for both temp professionals and Dental offices. Our platform is designed by dental professionals, for dental professionals which will continue to evolve with the insights, tips, and strategies that can only come from people who are using it.
That’s where the Kolligate Ignite ambassador program comes in.
About the Program
About the Program

Our approach is to harness influential leaders to create a niche group to inspire and appreciate diverse professionals on our platform reach their goals with ease and confidence. Our ambassadors are visionaries who are passionate to lead and contribute to the growth of the community. Through our program, ambassadors will be supported with preview apps, additions of standalone new features, share feedback and connect with a network of professionals as they channel positive influence through their own social media networks. These contributions create new opportunities for them and us to make a positive impact in our communities and help each other achieve our core set of values for which our ambassadors will be highly rewarded.

When you’re an Ignite Ambassador, you are not just a brand advocate, you are an inspiration to the Kolligate community and its users.
