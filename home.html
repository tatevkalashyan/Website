The kolligate Community
We value each and every member of the kolligate community and we are committed to promote satisfactory connections on our platform. The connections we facilitate have phenomenal outcomes since kolligate encourages positive behavior through promotions and reward programs. Whether you’re ready to earn more as a temp worker or on the lookout to fill an open shift, we invite you to join our kolligate community!

What We Offer
  • Interactive map to Discover shifts and available temps
  • Feature-rich smart Calendar
  • Manage all shifts through Shift Activity
  • Flexibility to Negotiate terms
  • Invite friends through Referrals
  • Export shift data from y Shift Histor

  • Share shifts with friends or on social media
  • Engagement opportunities for Externs/Volunteers
  • An option for Permanent Hiring
  • Positive Feedbackmodel
  • Real time alerts through kolligate Mobile App
  • A Community of professionals to help each other
  • Workflow designed for timely responses
